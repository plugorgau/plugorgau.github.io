Contact

The best way to contact PLUG is:

  • by e-mail; or
  • attend any of our in-person events in Western Australia - usually Perth, but many of us are up for a road trip to meet with our regional friends

If you are asking a Linux related question, you should subscribe to the PLUG email mailing list (free) and ask your question to the general members; you’re welcome to unsubscribe at any time should you wish to.

You can send the PLUG Committee an email committee@plug.org.au. The committee and its subcommittees are responsible for the administration and website of the association.
