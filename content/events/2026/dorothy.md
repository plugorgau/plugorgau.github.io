Benjamin Lupton talks about Dorothy. Dorothy is a dotfile ecosystem
with cross-platform automations. It supports numerous shells including
fish, zsh, and bash, and it runs on Mac and Linux. The presentation
gives an overview of the current state of the project and how it has
improved since Benjamin last spoke about it at PLUG in 2022.
